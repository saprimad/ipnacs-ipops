About the Conference

The 8th International Conference on Pharmaceutical, Nutraceutical, and Cosmeceutical Sciences (IPNaCS) and the 11th International Postgraduate Conference on Pharmaceutical Sciences (IPoPS) mark the 25th Anniversary of the Faculty of Pharmacy.

This milestone event brings together global collaborators to explore how digital transformation is reshaping pharmacology, pharmacy, nutraceuticals, and cosmetic science.

Conference Tracks

Pharmacology & Precision Medicine

AI-driven drug discovery, digital biomarkers, and tailored therapeutic strategies.

Pharmacy & Digital Health

Telepharmacy, blockchain supply chains, smart dispensing, IoMT, and gamified platforms.

Nutraceutical Science

Personalised nutrition, wearable-linked interventions, and preventive health.

Cosmetic Science & Innovation

AI-powered formulation, virtual diagnostics, and sustainable product development.

Pharmaceutical Technology

Advanced drug delivery systems, continuous manufacturing, biopharmaceutical production, and QbD approaches.

Pharmaceutical Chemistry

Medicinal chemistry, green synthesis, computational modelling, and analytical validation.

Regenerative Medicine

Stem cells, tissue engineering, organoids, and pathways from lab to clinical application.

Policy, Regulation & Ethics

Global harmonisation of standards, data privacy, and ethical sourcing.
