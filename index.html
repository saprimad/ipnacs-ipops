About the Conference

[cite_start]The 8th International Conference on Pharmaceutical, Nutraceutical, and Cosmeceutical Sciences (IPNaCS) and the 11th International Postgraduate Conference on Pharmaceutical Sciences (IPoPS) marks the 25th Anniversary of the Faculty of Pharmacy[cite: 3, 9].

[cite_start]This milestone event will bring together global collaborators to explore how digital transformation is reshaping pharmacology, pharmacy, nutraceuticals, and cosmetic science[cite: 10].
